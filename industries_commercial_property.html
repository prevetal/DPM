To design commercial real estate, for example, business centers, offices, research centers, it is necessary to take into account the high requirements of safety, convenience and modern solutions.

It is important to create a functional and aesthetic space that will meet the needs of future tenants or visitors. When designing we take into account our Client’s wishes, location of future building, the features of the building site, lighting conditions, existing engineering communications, design features, transport accessibility, and so on.

It is also important to take into account modern technologies and trends, such as the possibility of remote work, online conferences, video surveillance systems and smart technologies for building management.
