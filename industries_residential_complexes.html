The design of residential complexes is a complicated process that requires a combination of architectural design, engineering solutions, safety and convenience for residents. When designing residential complexes, such aspects as the layout of residential premises, the infrastructure of the complex, energy efficiency of buildings, safety and environmental friendliness should be taken into account.

Architects and engineers strive to create residential complexes that combine functionality, attractive appearance, comfortable living conditions and modern technologies. They have to consider the needs of various groups of the people, such as young families, the elderly and children, and create residential complexes that meet their expectations.

They trust us

An important aspect in the design of residential complexes is also the organization of public spaces within the complex territory, such as parks, squares, sports grounds, shops and cafes. These spaces facilitate the communication of residents and create a cozy and comfortable environment.

In addition, when designing residential complexes, DPM team take into account the economic feasibility and stability of buildings to various adverse factors, such as weather conditions or man-made accidents, etc.

Thus, the design of residential complexes is multifaceted process that requires integration of various aspects to create a high-quality and comfortable living environment.

The DPM team offers a comprehensive approach to the implementation of projects of this type: design, as well as procurement and construction management.
