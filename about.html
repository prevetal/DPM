About DPM GLOBAL

Main – About us – About DPM GLOBAL
DPM is an architectural and engineering company that has been successfully implementing construction projects in Russia and abroad for more than 15 years.

DPM was founded in 2006 and has opened its portfolio with a number of engineering systems projects for residential and public buildings.

More than 100 different projects have been completed for today, including such as consulting in the selection of optimal land plots for construction, assessment of the current condition of buildings, creation of a concept for a future building, development of design and working documentation and project management services.

The DPM team consists of more than 80 professionals experienced in designing hotel complexes, public buildings and large industrial facilities. And, following the needs of the projects, our team continues to grow with new specialists.

Depending on the objectives of the project we may involve our project management team and site supervision specialists.

To optimize the project implementation process and minimize risks, DPM creates a BIM model of the facility.

All project participants – architects, engineers and the customer – work with a common information model, which ensures the unity and accuracy of the data.

The presence of a BIM model also simplifies communication and coordination between various contractors and specialists on the construction site.

The main mission of DPM is the implementation of turnkey ECPM projects. We offer our clients comprehensive construction services and our in-depth experience in this field.

THE BEST FOR YOU. THE BEST FOR THE PROJECT.

  • Variability, the best solutions that match all your wishes
  • Using optimal design solutions to reduce capital costs

  • Your round-the-clock access to the information model during project development
  • Absence of collisions in structures and utilities that negatively affect all stages of the project implementation
  • Model flyovers and rounds sessions with the Customer for greater immersion and understanding of the final product already at the design stage – the possibility of adjustments and improvements
Attention to detail:
  • Thoughtful planning solutions that ensure ergonomics and high efficiency of spaces
  • Selection of materials that ensure durability and aesthetics, easy to use
  • Application of best practices and knowledge earned at the previous projects
    Taking care of the subsequent stages of the project:
  • Professional selection of suppliers of equipment and materials, taking into account their availability on the market and the price-quality ratio
  • Detailing and layout of drawings, ensuring easy understanding by builders
  • A detailed 3D model that allows the operation of the building without using paper versions of the as-built documentation.
